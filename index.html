Biking in a big city is always an adventure — there are slow bikers, aggressive drivers and bike lanes that do not make any sense.

In this game, you need to navigate the craze of Berlin traffic:

  • If you crash into a car, the game is over
  • If you crash into a fellow bike rider, you lose your game points!
  • Collect some of the famous Berlin treats on the way to sustain yourself enough to climb all these city hills
